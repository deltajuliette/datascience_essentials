## Learning Objectives

*After this lesson, students will be able to:*

1. Describe generalized linear models.
2. Fit Poisson and Gamma regression models in `statsmodels`.
3. Interpret coefficients from Poisson and Gamma regression models.
4. Describe iteratively reweighted least squares.

---

## Student Requirements

*Before this lesson(s), students should already be able to:*

1. Fit linear and logistic regression models in `statsmodels`.
2. Interpret coefficients of linear and logistic regression models.
3. Describe the support of Poisson and Gamma distributions.

---

## Lesson Outline

> **Total Time: 90 mins**

Part I. **Introduction to Generalized Linear Models** (30 minutes total)
- Why linear models?
- Rewriting Normal linear regression
- Rewriting logistic regression
- Linear, link, and random components of GLMs

Part II. **Poisson and Gamma Regression** (45 minutes total)
- Defining Poisson model
- Fitting and interpreting Poisson model
- Defining Gamma model
- Fitting and interpreting Gamma model

Part III. **Iteratively Reweighted Least Squares** (15 minutes total)
